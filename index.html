Welcome !

Garden City is a school, where excellence meets innovation! We offer a wide range of subjects and extracurricular programs designed to nurture every student’s potential.

Subjects Offered

Mathematics

Core Subject

Mathematics helps students develop logical reasoning and problem-solving skills. Topics include algebra, geometry, statistics, and real-world applications that prepare students for higher-level learning and critical thinking.

Art & Design

Elective

Art & Design encourages creativity and visual expression. Students explore drawing, painting, digital design, and other mediums to develop both technical skills and imaginative thinking.

English Literature

Core Subject

English Literature develops reading comprehension, writing, and analytical skills. Students study poetry, novels, and plays, learning to interpret texts and express ideas clearly and creatively.

Music

Elective

Music introduces students to performing, composing, and understanding musical theory. They learn rhythm, melody, and harmony while developing appreciation for various musical styles and instruments.

Information Technology

Core Subject

Information Technology teaches students how to use computers, software applications, and basic coding skills. They learn to solve problems digitally and develop skills essential for modern education and the workplace.

Business Studies

Elective

Business Studies introduces students to entrepreneurship, management, and finance. They explore marketing strategies, business planning, and real-world economic concepts in a practical, hands-on way.

Clubs & Programs

Science Club

Hands-on experiments, science fairs, and competitions to spark curiosity.

Art & Culture Club

Painting, music, drama, and cultural performances to nurture creativity.

Internships

Work with local businesses and organizations to gain real-world experience.

Scholarships

Early registration scholarships for top-performing students each year.
