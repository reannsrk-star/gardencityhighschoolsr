About the Art & Culture Club

The Art & Culture Club at Garden City School encourages creativity, self-expression, and appreciation for the arts. Students engage in painting, music, drama, and cultural performances to explore their talents and showcase their skills.

Activities

  • Weekly painting, drawing, and sculpture workshops
  • Music rehearsals and choir sessions
  • Drama and theater productions
  • Cultural celebrations and performances
  • Art exhibitions and talent showcases

Benefits

  • Enhances creativity and artistic skills
  • Boosts confidence through performances and presentations
  • Encourages collaboration and teamwork
  • Opportunities to participate in competitions and exhibitions
  • Fosters cultural awareness and appreciation for diversity

How to Join

Membership is open to all students from Grades 7–12. To join, complete the registration form and submit it to the Art & Culture Club coordinator.
