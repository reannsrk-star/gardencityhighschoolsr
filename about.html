About Us

Garden City School is dedicated to providing high-quality education in a safe, supportive, and inspiring environment. We aim to help students grow academically, socially, and personally so they can become confident and responsible individuals.

Our school offers a balanced curriculum that includes core subjects such as Mathematics, English, Science, and Information Technology, along with electives like Art, Music, and Business Studies. Our experienced teachers use engaging and modern teaching methods to encourage critical thinking, creativity, and a lifelong love for learning.

At Garden City, learning goes beyond the classroom. Students can participate in clubs, sports, internships, workshops, and leadership programs that help develop teamwork, discipline, and real-world skills.

We believe in strong partnerships between the school, parents, and the community. Together, we create a positive environment where every student feels valued, motivated, and prepared for a successful future.
