About the Science Club

The Science Club at Garden City School is designed to ignite curiosity and foster a love of discovery. Students explore a wide range of scientific fields, participate in hands-on experiments, and develop critical thinking skills.

Activities

  • Weekly hands-on experiments in biology, chemistry, and physics
  • Annual science fairs and exhibitions
  • Competitions like science quizzes, robotics, and coding challenges
  • Field trips to labs, research centers, and science museums
  • Workshops on astronomy, renewable energy, and environmental science

Benefits

  • Enhances problem-solving and critical thinking skills
  • Encourages teamwork and collaboration
  • Prepares students for science competitions and STEM careers
  • Builds confidence in presenting experiments and projects
  • Opportunity to interact with scientists and experts through guest lectures

How to Join

Membership is open to all students from Grades 7–12. To join, complete the registration form and submit it to the Science Club coordinator by the end of the month.
