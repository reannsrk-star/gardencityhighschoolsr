About Our Internship Program

Garden City School offers students the opportunity to participate in real-world internships with local businesses, organizations, and institutions. Our internships provide practical experience, develop professional skills, and prepare students for future careers.

Activities

  • Hands-on experience in professional workplaces
  • Shadowing industry professionals and mentors
  • Participation in company projects and team tasks
  • Workshops on resumes, interviews, and workplace skills
  • Networking opportunities with professionals and alumni

Benefits

  • Gain practical, real-world experience in chosen fields
  • Develop professional and soft skills like communication and teamwork
  • Explore potential career paths before graduation
  • Receive mentorship from industry experts
  • Enhance college and job applications with internship experience

How to Apply

Internship applications are open to all eligible persons. Complete the registration form and provide the required documents for consideration. Submit your application to the school's administrative office.
