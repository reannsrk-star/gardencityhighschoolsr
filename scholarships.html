About Scholarships at Garden City

Garden City School provides scholarships to outstanding students who demonstrate academic excellence, leadership, or exceptional talents in arts and sports. Scholarships aim to support students in reaching their full potential while reducing financial barriers.

Types of Scholarships

  • Academic Excellence Scholarships for top-performing students
  • Leadership Scholarships for students demonstrating outstanding leadership skills
  • Arts & Music Scholarships for exceptional talent in creative disciplines
  • Sports Scholarships for students excelling in athletics
  • Need-Based Scholarships for students requiring financial support

Benefits

  • Financial support to ease tuition and school fees
  • Recognition for excellence and achievement
  • Opportunities to develop talents and leadership skills
  • Boost college applications and resumes
  • Motivation to strive for continued academic and personal growth

How to Apply

Scholarship applications are open to all eligible students. Complete the registration form and provide the required documents for consideration. Submit your application to the school's administrative office.
