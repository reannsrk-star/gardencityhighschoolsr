Admissions

Garden City School welcomes students who are motivated to learn and grow in a supportive academic environment. Our admissions process is designed to be simple and accessible for all families.

Admission Requirements

Applicants must submit the following documents:

  • Completed Registration Form
  • Copy of Birth Certificate
  • Two Passport-Size Photos
  • Latest School Report or Transcript
  • Proof of Address

Fee Structure

Item Amount (BZD)
Registration Fee $50
Tuition (per term) $300
Activity Fee $40
Technology Fee $35

Special Offers

Early registration discounts are available for students who apply before the deadline. Scholarships are also offered to high-achieving students and those demonstrating financial need.
